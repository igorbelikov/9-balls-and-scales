9 balls and scales

You have 9 balls, equally big, equally heavy - except for one, which is a little heavier.

How would you identify the heavier ball if you could use a pair of balance scales only twice?

Step 1

You can change "heavy" ball yourself (click on the ball).
Click on the "Next step" button to continue

Step 2

Now we divide the balls into 3 groups.

Step 3

Now we compare the first 2 groups (first comparison).
Groups are equal, so heavy ball is in the 3rd group.
This group is heavier, so the heavy ball in it.

Step 4

Now we weigh heavier group. (second comparison).
Balls are equal, so heavy ball is the 3rd of current group.
This group is heavier, so the heavy ball in it.

Step 5

Result: heavy ball is number 0
Congratulations! :-)
